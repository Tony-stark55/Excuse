What is this?

ExcuseMe is an AI-powered excuse generator built for students, professionals, and anyone who has ever needed a quick, convincing reason for... well, anything. Whether you missed a lecture, forgot a deadline, or just needed a day off — we've got you covered in English and Hinglish.

Why we built it

Let's be honest — life gets messy. Sometimes you need a well-worded excuse fast, and your brain just isn't cooperating. ExcuseMe takes the stress out of that moment. Pick your situation, pick your vibe, and let the AI do the awkward part. No judgment. We've all been there.
